The Fundamental Styles of Tango with Ignacio Varchausky

Posted by DJ Lorenzo on 2018-05-13
In Gigs, Music
Tags gigs, tango_music

Do not miss the seminar with Ignacio Varchausky Friday May 25 at Kavakava Tango!!!

Ignacio is well known as member of Orquesta El Arranque, and for his work on transmitting knowledge of tango music to the new generation of tango musicians through Orquesta Escuela de Tango and TangoVia. He just released the book The Bass in Tango in the series “Método de tango”.

Transmitting tango knowledge to a wider audience is of great importance to Ignacio, and he has a weekly radio show at the Buenos Aires tango radio La 2x4.

Now he is ready for Oslo, and will give a seminar Friday May 25, just before the milonga at Kavakava. This seminar will be in the style of his popular series of seminars Los Estílos Fundamentales del Tango, only much more accessible to us as it will be given in English!

Welcome!

PS: In related news, after a one year hiatus, the music selection at the milonga will be by yours truly.
