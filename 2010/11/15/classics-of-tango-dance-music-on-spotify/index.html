Classics of Tango Dance Music on Spotify

Posted by DJ Lorenzo on 2010-11-15
In
Tags spotify, tangomusic

To help beginners and other interested in getting acquainted with the classics of tango music, I started putting together Spotify playlists from Stephen Brown’s excellent list Classics of Tango Dance Music. This is a work in progress, and it is not all in there. These playlists are all collaborative playlists, and any help is very much appreciated. I got a few Spotify invites to spare, let me know if you need one.

At the time of writing, the playlists for Milonga, Vals and Early Golden Age are mostly done.

EDIT: Thanks to swingsmurfen for adding a lot of tracks to the Old Guard list.
